WHAT WE DO

You don’t have to be a millionaire to make a difference. Even the smallest of donations have the potential to improve a person’s life. LifeStock is a free donation web app that allows you to accumulate your spare change and donate it in the form of stock. Just link up your credit card, debit card, or bank account and we’ll take care of the rest!

HOW LIFESTOCK WORKS

We’ve partnered up with top firms willing to sell their stock at a 10% reduced rate for charities. Other firms give the option to wave their brokerage fee. Every time you make a purchase, we’ll round it up to the nearest dollar. Watch your spare change accumulate, invest in your favorite company’s stock, and donate to a charity of your choice. Additionally, since you’re trying to improve society, you can avoid paying capital gains taxes on the stock gains. Spend away!
